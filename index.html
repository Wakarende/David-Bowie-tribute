David Bowie

Story of the man who sold the world


David Robert Jones known as Dvid Bowie, was an English singer, songwriter and actor. His career was marked by reinvention and visual representation, with is music and stagecraft having a significant impact on popular music. During his lifetime, his record slaes, estimated at 140 million albums worldwide, made him one of the world's best-selling music artists.
